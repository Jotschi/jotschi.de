Do you want to extract one specific frame from a movie using mplayer?

[source, bash]
---- 
mplayer -ss [second] -frames 1 -vo png [moviefile]
----

Example:

[source, bash]
---- 
mplayer -ss 0.04166666667 -frames 1 -vo png test.mov
----
